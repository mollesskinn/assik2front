Nijiro Murakami

About Nijiro

Nijiro Murakami (born 1997) is a Japanese actor and voice actor best known internationally for his role as Shuntaro Chishiya in the Netflix series Alice in Borderland.

Major Life Events

  1. 1997 – Born in Tokyo, Japan
  2. Childhood – Lived with mother (singer UA) after parents’ divorce
  3. Teen years – Studied partly in Okinawa and in Montreal, Canada
  4. 2014 – Film debut in Still the Water, premiered at Cannes
  5. 2015 – TV debut in Tenshi no Naifu and Anohana live-action
  6. 2020–2022 – Gained worldwide recognition in Alice in Borderland

Key Contributions

  • Acclaimed role in Still the Water (2014)
  • Award-winning performance in Destruction Babies (2016)
  • Voice acting in anime Inuyashiki and film Isle of Dogs
  • Breakthrough global fame with Alice in Borderland
